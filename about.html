About Reign Academy

Cultivating Elegance, Leadership, and Purpose

Reign Pageant Academy was founded with a singular vision: to redefine what it means to be a beauty queen in the modern era. We noticed a gap in the industry—many aspiring queens had the beauty and drive but lacked the refined skills, strategic branding, and mental resilience needed to win international titles and sustain a career thereafter.

We are Kenya’s premier finishing and pageant development institution, a sanctuary where raw potential is polished into regal excellence.

Our Philosophy

The "Reign" Standard

We believe that royalty is not just a title you win; it is a mindset you cultivate. Our training methodology is holistic, focusing on three pillars:

Refinement

Mastering the art of etiquette, poise, and social graces to navigate any room with confidence.

Intellect

Developing critical thinking, public speaking, and advocacy skills to amplify your voice and cause.

Purpose

Aligning your personal brand with a cause that truly matters, ensuring your reign leaves a lasting legacy.

Meet the Director

Audrey Rendo (Founder & Head Coach)

Audrey Rendo is an accomplished runway model, fashion blogger, and media science graduate who represented Kenya at Miss Progress International 2018 in Italy. With a rich background in pageantry—including titles like Miss Moi University and Miss Talent—she founded Reign Pageant Academy to bridge the gap between raw potential and international success.

"My goal is to see every student walk out of our doors not just as a contestant, but as a leader ready to take on the world."
